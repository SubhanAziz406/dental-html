Our story

Dentistry built around trust, not just teeth.

Lahore Dental Clinic started as a single treatment room in Lahore and grew, appointment by appointment, into a full family practice — without ever losing the personal feel that got us here.

Why we do things differently

Most clinics are built around the dentist's schedule. We built ours around the patient's nerves — the ones nobody talks about until they're sitting in the chair.

"A good appointment is one the patient barely has to think about afterwards."

That means clear explanations before any drill touches a tooth, real answers about cost, and enough time built into every slot that no one feels rushed out the door.

How we got here

A decade of small, deliberate growth

Year 1

One room, one chair

Opened with general checkups and fillings, seeing a handful of patients a day by referral only.

Year 4

Orthodontics added

Brought in braces and early alignment care as more families asked for treatment under one roof.

Year 8

Implants & sedation options

Expanded into implant dentistry and comfort-focused sedation for anxious and pediatric patients.

Today

A full family practice

Same-day emergency slots, digital records, and a team that now sees three generations of some families.

What guides us

We believe good dental care starts with honesty — telling you what actually needs treatment, and what can wait. It continues with comfort, because a tense patient is a harder patient to treat well. And it holds together through consistency — the same dentist, the same team, the same standard of care at every single visit, whether it's a routine cleaning or an emergency at 7pm on a Thursday.
