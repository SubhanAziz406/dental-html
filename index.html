Why we do things differently

Most clinics are built around the dentist's schedule. We built ours around the patient's nerves — the ones nobody talks about until they're sitting in the chair.

"A good appointment is one the patient barely has to think about afterwards."

That means clear explanations before any drill touches a tooth, real answers about cost, and enough time built into every slot that no one feels rushed out the door.
